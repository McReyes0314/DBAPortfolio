Process

In this activity, we are assigned to answer various questions about database administration, covering database administration definition and roles. After that, we will write our answers on a yellow pad paper. Here are the questions we answered in this activity:

  • What is data administration, and why is it significant in the management of database systems? What are the key responsibilities of a data administrator?
  • What are the primary responsibilities of a Database Administrator (DBA)? How does the DBA role evolve with the increasing complexity of database environments and technologies?
  • Why is data security important in database management? What are some common strategies or best practices that DBAs can implement to protect sensitive data from unauthorized access?
  • What is data availability, and why is it critical for organizations? What are three methods that DBAs can use to ensure high availability of database systems?
  • What does data quality mean in the context of database administration? What are the key dimensions of data quality, and how can a DBA monitor and improve data quality within an organization?

Learnings

Throughout the activity, I learned the following aspects

  • The importance of database administration and its key responsibilities.
  • The importance of database security and its strategies to keep sensitive data from unthorized access.
